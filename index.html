Intro

ELITE BJJ is an established martial arts club located at Tolpits Lane, Watford, delivering quality Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu classes in a state of the art facility.

We have experienced friendly instructors, who have been delivering popular classes for many years.

Whatever your goal, if you want to improve your fitness, learn self-defence, compete, or just have fun, ELITE BJJ can accommodate.

You are welcome to come along for a free trial class to see if Jiu-Jitsu is for you!

Why Join?

Strength & Fitness

BJJ is a great way to improve your overall strength and fitness, as classes involve a mixture of learning techniques, conditioning, and sparring.

Self Defence

BJJ teaches practical, real-life self defence skills, which are designed to be effective, no matter your size or strength.

Fun

Learning BJJ is hugely enjoyable, the variety of techniques to learn, along with the oppurtunity to test yourself and practice your skills in sparring sessions is great fun.
